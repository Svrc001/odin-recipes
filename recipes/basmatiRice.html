Basmati Rice Recipe

What is Basmati Rice?

Basmati Rice is a savory Indian rice flavored with toasted and fragrant whole spices and fried onions. Soaking the basmati rice before cooking makes all the difference. Serve with your favorite Indian curry or dal.

Now that we have learnt something about the dish, let's get to cooking!

Ingredients

Let's dive into the list of ingredients needed for this tasty dish.

  • Basmati rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cinnamon and Cardamom
  • Cloves
  • Onion
  • Cumin seeds

Steps

Now that we have gathered our ingredients, let's start cooking.

  1. Soak the rice: Place rice into a bowl with enough water to cover. Set aside to soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Cook spices: In the last 10 minutes of soaking, heat oil in a large pot or saucepan over medium heat. Add cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves, then stir in cumin seed.
  3. Cook onions: Cook and stir until fragrant and toasted, about a minute. Add sliced onion and stir.
  4. Cook rice: Drain water from rice, and stir into the pot. Cook and stir rice until lightly toasted, about 3 minutes.
  5. Add water and salt: Add water and salt, and bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to low. Simmer until all the water has been absorbed, about 15 minutes.

Now the dish is ready, enjoy!
